About Us

Local Attractions

  • Our website is dedicated to providing you with easy access to local activities and businesses in your area.

  • If you are visiting a place for the first time BuisnessEco will be able to provide you with information of smaller more local businesses from your area as well as larger brands to give you a wide range of things to do in your time away

Accounts

  • For the seasoned traveller or people looking to visit many places, your activty prefrences can be saved with an account.

  • With an account you can save your favourite places and activities you find to make it easier to find them again.

Random Activity Generator

  • Also there is an option to take a short quiz and depending on your answers it will alter your randomly generated activities to something more suited to your interests.

  • For example if you are a fan of the outdoors and nature, the quiz will ask you questions about your interests and then the activities will be tailored to your interests.

  • However if you choose not to take the quiz the random generator can still give you options but completely randomly.
